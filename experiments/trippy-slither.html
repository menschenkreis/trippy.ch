How to Play

⬅️➡️Use arrow keys or A/D to steer left/right.
📱Enable tilt to steer with your device, or drag on screen.
✨Collect glowing orbs to grow your trail and increase your score.
🌀Avoid your own trail — self-collision ends the run.
💨Speed gently increases as you collect more orbs.
