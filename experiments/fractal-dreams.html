Interpretation

The Julia set is a mathematical window into infinity. This piece uses a DMT-inspired palette and a harmonic Shepard tone to create a meditative, "zen" drift through geometry.

How it's done

A WebGL fragment shader calculates millions of iterations per second on your GPU. The audio is a 12-voice harmonic engine tuned to 432Hz.
