Falling Emy

Canvas 2D · Verlet Physics · Sacred Geometry

How to play

👆Drag the ragdoll by any body part
📱Tilt your phone to shift gravity
+Add more ragdolls to the void
✦Theme cycles sacred geometry styles
⟳Reset clears everything

What's happening

Emy and procedurally generated friends fall through an infinite void filled with frictionless spheres. Grab them to freeze time and reposition them. Sacred geometry patterns (flower of life, Metatron's cube, golden spirals) pulse softly in the background.
