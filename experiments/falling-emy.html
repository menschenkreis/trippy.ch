The Journey

We all fall through time. Sometimes we drift, sometimes we crash. Falling Emy is a meditation on the descent — the frictionless movement through the obstacles, challenges, and heartbreaks that define a life. Emy represents a variant of Emanuel's being, falling through an infinite void.
