How to Play

⬅️➡️Use arrow keys or A/D to move left/right.
📱Enable tilt to steer with your device, or tap left/right side of screen.
🔄Player wraps around screen edges.
⬆️Jump higher and higher. Don't fall below!

Platform Types

Normal — Solid neon platform
Fragile — Breaks after one bounce
Moving — Drifts horizontally
Spring — Super boost jump!
Vanishing — Fades after landing
