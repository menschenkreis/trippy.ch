What you're seeing

A flight through infinite procedural space filled with morphing fractal geometry — breathing shapes, rippling rings, and floating orbs. Everything feels alive.

How it's rendered

Ray marching with a signed distance function (SDF). The SDF computes the exact distance to the nearest surface, allowing efficient traversal. Each step the camera descends deeper, and the fractal iteration count adapts. Psychedelic colour is derived from orbit traps and surface normals.
